The Davis Family Chronicle

From Kent, England to New England  ·  1600 to the present

Compiled from the 1881 research of Horace Davis and supplemented by family documents, this chronicle traces the Davis family line from its origins in the Weald of Kent, England through its generations of New England settlement — a story of carpenters, farmers, soldiers, ministers, and governors.
