Richard Prescott Davis III — "Tripp"
R3
Richard Prescott Davis III
born November 15, 2021  ·  son of Rick & Jillian Davis  ·  Carlisle, Massachusetts
Richard Prescott Davis III — known as Tripp — is the first child of Richard Prescott Davis II and Jillian Romano Davis, born November 15, 2021 at Emerson Hospital in Concord, Massachusetts. He is the fourth consecutive Richard Prescott Davis in the family. The name carries from grandfather (Richard Prescott Davis), through father (Richard Prescott Davis II), to this newest generation. He lives in Carlisle, Massachusetts, with his parents and younger sister Isabelle.
Haven James Davis
HJ
Haven James Davis
born December 13, 2022, Hanover, New Hampshire  ·  son of Collin & Donna Davis
Haven James Davis was born on December 13, 2022, at Dartmouth Hitchcock Medical Center in Hanover, New Hampshire — the same hospital where his father Collin was born. He lives with his parents Collin and Donna in Sarasota, Florida. Haven is the fifteenth generation in a line that began when Dolor Davis arrived in Cambridge, Massachusetts in 1634 — nearly four centuries of American history compressed into one unbroken family thread.
Isabelle Anne Davis
IA
Isabelle Anne Davis
born June 3, 2023  ·  daughter of Rick & Jillian Davis  ·  Carlisle, Massachusetts
Isabelle Anne Davis was born on June 3, 2023, at home in Carlisle, Massachusetts — the second child of Richard Prescott Davis II and Jillian Romano Davis. She lives in Carlisle, Massachusetts with her parents and older brother Tripp.

From Dolor Davis — a house-carpenter from the Weald of Kent, arriving in Cambridge, Massachusetts in 1634 with nothing but a trade and a family — to Haven James Davis, Tripp Davis, and Isabelle Anne Davis, born in Hanover, Carlisle, and beyond in 2021–2023. Fifteen generations. Nearly four centuries. One unbroken line.
